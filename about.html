    Wills and Estates

    We provide a custom-tailor made Wills for our clients. Every persons' circumstances are different and therefore the one fits all solution that many lawyers provide does not always necessary fulfill all clients requirements. Unfortunately, many lawyers do also ignore the fact that these transactions are not just property related transactions for individuals but are really the most important decisions one makes when dealing family matters. These decisions often involve existing and future assets and how these assets are to be split, but the drafting of a will also involves the naming of Guardians for minor children. We always listen to our clients and start with careful analysis with our company invented custom-built solutions by usually offering three different scenarios: minimal,optimal and maximum solutions to organize for our Clients future Estates needs which may include the services of other professionals.
